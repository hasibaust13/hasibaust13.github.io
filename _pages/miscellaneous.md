### Photo - Remove Background

* **Adobe Photoshop:** Straightforward with generative AI prompting. To use another picture's background, remove the subject while keeping the background and fill the gap with generative AI. After placing the subject on the new background, match colour exposures to make the editing smooth.
* [Retoucher](https://retoucher.online/){:target="_blank"}: Variety of background options, different size options for downloading. No sign-up required for the first three downloads.
* [Fotor](https://www.fotor.com/){:target="_blank"}: Preview (lower quality) can be downloaded without sign up. Has similar features as above.
