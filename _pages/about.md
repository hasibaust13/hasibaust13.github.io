# Greetings!

I am **Khan Md Hasib**, a second year PhD Student in the School of Systems and Computing at the [University of New South Wales (UNSW)](https://www.unsw.edu.au/canberra){:target="_blank"}, Canberra, Australia. At present, I am investigating the development and application of artificial intelligence methods, particularly knowledge graph blending with machine learning and large language models, to improve health systems and outcomes.

Prior to that I worked as an **Assistant Professor** at Department of Computer Science and Engineering, [Bangladesh University of Business and Technology (BUBT)](https://www.bubt.edu.bd){:target="_blank"}, Dhaka, Bangladesh. I have received my M.Sc. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, [Brac University](https://www.bracu.ac.bd/){:target="_blank"}, Dhaka, Bangladesh. I also have completed my B.Sc. from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, [Ahsanullah University of Science & Technology (AUST)](https://www.aust.edu/){:target="_blank"}, Dhaka, Bangladesh.

I am open to research collaborations in the fields of **Machine Learning**, **Medical Imaging**, **Explainable AI**, **Natural Language Processing**, and **Health Informatics**. If you are interested in collaborating or exploring research opportunities, I would be pleased to connect.
